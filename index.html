Project Proposal

Vision Statement

This project will give me the skills and knowledge to start using my computer science skills to build a business and contribute to communities I care about (healthcare & disability). This program provides incredible education for the development part of computer science, but appropriately not as much on the skills that transfer coding into a useful product. Because I would like to run my own business someday, I would like to take this time to focus on the skills that transform coding skills into something that can benefit and support people.

Motivation

This project is personally relevant to me because I will be using this website as both a public image and launching point for a web and application development business. Whether or not I will do this full-time or on the side of my full-time position, I am not sure. However, I do know that I would like to have a wide diversity of projects so that I can continue to learn new skills and interact with new clients. In addition, I enjoy helping clients to determine the best process and technology for their purpose.

Timeline

- Project Exploration (1 hr)
- Project Proposal (1 hr)
- Web Development Exploration and Summary (10 hrs)
- Layout and Content Exploration and Summary (5 hrs)
- Business Research (5 hrs)
- Mentorship (2 hrs)
- Build Website (15 hrs)
- Feedback, Testing, and Final Changes (5 hrs)
- Final Project Writeup (1 hr)

Risks

- Finding a mentor may be difficult and unpredictable
- The site will require a new environment which may require an unpredictable amount of support

Mitigation

If I am unable to find a mentor to meet with, I have a few professors that I have good relationships with that I can reach out to if necessary. However, ideally I would like to form a new relationship. I will reach out to the professor of this course for assistance if I am really struggling with the environment

Evaluation Criteria

1. List of specific tasks required to set up a site
2. List of recommended design & functionality standards
3. Design, theme, and written content in professional format
4. Meeting with 1 mentor with documented feedback on #1-3
5. Live site available to the public
